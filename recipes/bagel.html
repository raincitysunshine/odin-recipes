Sunshine Sandwiches on Mount Royal Bagels

aka Sunshine Sandwiches in the Kelowna Style

Description

Being a sandwich freak, I was delighted to find out that Kelowna has some really rich foundations for the perfect sandwich.

The following is an example of how to construct the perfect bagel sandwich using Mount Royal bagels, the gold standard of holey goodness in the Okanagan.

Ingredients

  • Mount Royal Bagels
  • cheese--Philadelphia Cream Cheese for the anchovy version or Dubliner for the eggy version
  • The farm eggs procured at Three Lakes Brewing
  • Mustard for the egg version
  • fancy anchovies from Spain, the ones that cose $7 a tin
  • Scallions

Steps

  1. Toast the bagel
  2. While it's toasting, fry the egg if making the eggy version
  3. Get all the other ingredients ready
  4. Spread and schmear away
  5. Top with scallions
